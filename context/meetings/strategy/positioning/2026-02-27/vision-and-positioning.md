# Positioning Session — Vision & Positioning — February 27, 2026

---

## What DD Is Becoming

DD is not a pick server. It is a full-stack sports betting education platform built as a community. Members get:
- The EV alerts and methodology DD uses to find edges
- The educational framework to understand, evaluate, and apply those edges themselves
- Bankroll and strategy tools to bet sustainably and profitably long-term
- Partnerships to act on those edges across sportsbooks and DFS apps

The distinction: picks are examples of the methodology. The methodology is the product.

> "I hate course selling. I'm never going to course sell. I'd rather build a community."

---

## Why Members Weren't Profitable (Even When Analysts Were)

The problem wasn't the picks — it was volume and education. DD was sending too many picks, and members didn't have the framework to use them correctly. They were gambling, not betting. They didn't know how to:
- Size their bets relative to their bankroll
- Limit daily exposure
- Handle variance

The fix is not fewer analysts or different picks. The fix is education — teaching members how to interact with the picks they're given.
