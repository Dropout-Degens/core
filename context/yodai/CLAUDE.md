# YodAI — AI Layer & Voice Notes\n\nThis folder holds AI-generated outputs, improvement suggestions, and processed voice notes. It's a working area for AI-produced content that may inform future decisions—nothing here is canonical business doctrine.\n\n---\n\n## Folder Structure\n\n### `improvements/`\nSpecific improvement suggestions for AI features, bot behavior, and operational efficiency. Each file proposes a change with reasoning.\n\n- `open/` — improvements under consideration or in progress\n- `implemented/` — improvements that have been built and deployed\n\n**Go here when:** reviewing previously drafted AI improvements, checking what's been built, or proposing new optimization ideas.\n\n---\n\n### `voice-notes/`\nProcessed transcriptions and briefs from Dax's voice notes, organized by type:\n\n- `brainstorms/` — exploratory thinking, \"what if\" scenarios, and hypothesis testing\n- `ideas/` — specific proposals or feature/process concepts\n- `thoughts/` — reflections, meta-observations, and strategic musings\n\nEach voice note produces two files:\n1. **Transcript** — full verbatim transcription + clarifying questions asked + raw response\n2. **Brief** — 2-3 sentence summary, key points, context from Q&A, and next steps\n\n**Go here when:** retrieving past context on a topic, continuing work from a previous session, or looking for inspiration on how something was originally framed.\n\n---\n\n### Other Directories\n\n- `alg-research/` — algorithm research and analysis notes\n- `calls/` — call-related documentation and summaries\n- `implemented/` — archive of completed implementations\n\n---\n\n## How to Use This Folder\n\n**Reading context:** Always check for existing voice notes or improvement suggestions before starting work on a topic. Use `load_past_context` if you need a quick summary of past sessions.\n\n**Processing voice notes:** When a voice note comes in as a brainstorm, idea, or thought stream, classify it, ask 3 clarifying questions, wait for a reply, then create both transcript and brief files on the daily branch.\n\n**Creating improvements:** When drafting improvement suggestions, save them to `improvements/open/` with a clear problem statement, proposed solution, and reasoning. Move to `implemented/` once built.\n\n**Merging to main:** Daily branch work gets reviewed at the 11:30 PM recap and merged if approved.\n"
