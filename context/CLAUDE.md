# Dropout Degens — Context Folder

This folder is the central knowledge base for Dropout Degens. Everything an AI needs to understand the business, its people, its decisions, and its outputs lives here.

Three subfolders. Each has its own CLAUDE.md or README with a full breakdown.

---

## `meetings/`
All processed notes from every meeting Dax has had — internal team, external contacts, developer syncs, and solo strategy sessions. Organized by category and person, broken into focused files by topic.

**Read `meetings/CLAUDE.MD` first.** It contains the full folder map (down to person/title level), descriptions of every file type, and instructions for how to navigate and process meetings.

**Go here when:** you need context on a specific person or relationship, want to understand how a decision was made, need to catch up on open action items, or are processing a new meeting.

---

## `operations/`
Standing business documentation — how DD operates day to day. Covers business rules, staff structure, compensation, onboarding, data pipeline, and routine operational context.

**Read `operations/CLAUDE.md` first** for the full business context, role framing, and philosophy. Then navigate into subfolders as needed:

- `business/` — analyst requirements, brand voice, compensation structures, conversion bonus system, revenue allocation, content guides, welcome ticket messaging
- `staff-onboarding/` — onboarding overview and staff bonus guide
- `data/` — EV alert data pipeline, import process, analysis findings
- `developing/` — developer-facing documentation
- `routine/` — recurring operational context (being built out)

**Go here when:** you need to know how something works operationally, are drafting staff-facing content, need business rules or compensation details, or are working with the data pipeline.

---

## `yodai/`
AI-generated outputs — ideas, improvements, and voice note transcriptions produced by or for the AI layer of DD's operations. Nothing here is a canonical business decision; it's a holding area for AI-produced content that may inform future decisions.

- `improvements/` — specific improvement suggestions for AI features and bot behavior (e.g., Discord channel mapping, token limit handling, timezone standardization)
- `voice-notes/` — transcribed or processed voice notes from Dax, organized by type: `brainstorms/`, `ideas/`, `thoughts/`

**Go here when:** you are reviewing previously captured ideas, looking for improvement suggestions that were already drafted, or storing new AI-generated output.
